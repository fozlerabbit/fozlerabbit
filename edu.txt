Hi, my name is Fozle Rabbi, and I am from Mymensingh, Bangladesh. I am a science student, a programmer, a youth leader, and someone who genuinely believes that education has the power to change not just one life but entire communities. Thank you for giving me this space to share my story.
I grew up in a city that is not on most global maps, but the problems I saw around me were very much global problems. Children dropping out of school. Young people with no access to technology. Communities where a girl's future was decided before she even had a chance to dream. I did not wait for someone else to respond. I started building solutions from the ground up.
I founded ScriptySphere, a youth-led initiative that has reached over 100,000 students across Bangladesh through civic technology and education programmes.
What drew me to Augustana College is the way it thinks about education. Augustana is not just focused on what you know when you graduate. It is focused on who you become. The liberal arts model, the close faculty relationships, the emphasis on research and community engagement, these are exactly the things I have been living outside of a classroom for the past several years. Augustana feels like the place where my practical experience and academic ambition can finally grow together in the same direction.
My goal is to study Computer Science and use that foundation to build technology that serves people who are most often left out of the conversation. Artificial intelligence for education in underserved communities. Digital tools for social organisations. Platforms that give young people in places like Mymensingh the same access to opportunity that students in major cities take for granted.
I will not just be a student at Augustana. I will be someone who shows up, contributes, connects, and challenges those around me to think bigger. I have spent my teenage years proving that one person with a clear purpose can create meaningful change. I am ready to bring that same energy to your campus.
Thank you for listening. I hope to join your community soon.

Hi, my name is Fozle Rabbi, and I am applying to Augustana College from Mymensingh, Bangladesh. Thank you for giving me this opportunity to share my financial circumstances with you.
My primary sponsor is my family. My father works in the private sector and my family has consistently supported my education throughout my academic journey. However, like many middle-income families in Bangladesh, fully covering the cost of an international education is beyond what we can manage independently.
Based on Augustana's estimated cost of attendance. My family is prepared to contribute approximately 25,000 USD annually, which reflects a genuine and serious financial commitment on their part.
That said, i am hoping for the highest possible scholarship. 
I want to be completely transparent with you. I am not looking for a free ride. I am looking for a fair opportunity. I have spent the last several years building organisations, mentoring students, competing nationally and internationally, and creating real social impact in Bangladesh. I bring that same work ethic and commitment to everything I do.
Augustana College is not just a financial decision for me. It is the right academic environment where I believe I can grow, contribute, and make the most of the opportunity being offered.
I hope you will consider my request seriously. Thank you so much for your time.
